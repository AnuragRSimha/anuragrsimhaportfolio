My Interest in Literature, Linguistics, and Music

Story and creative writing have always been an integral part of me since I was a kid. To while my time, I have always engaged in writing short stories, novels, and as a kid, poems. My deep-rooted interest in literature sailed me on the boat to the world of linguistics, where my interest in learning languages and exploring the world began. I love analyzing the origins of languages, particularly the languages of the European family. The Germanic and Romance language families are my favorite! Language is something that helps build personality, and music is something that helps shape it better. Whenever there is absolute silence, listening to music brings me great satisfaction. I simply enjoy listening to classical music by celebrated artists like Mozart, Beethoven, Sabastian Bach, etc. Old Hollywood classics and noir jazz are my top favorite generes of music besides classical music. These three divine forms of art have always been important to me.
