History as My Hobby

I am huge fan of history! Particularly, the history of the western hemisphere. Whenever I find time, I allow myself to delve into learning about various events from the past and analyze the dynamics then versus now. While many people might find history as a boring topic, in truth, it's an extremely exciting subject of study. From the time of the Egyptian Civilization, to the Roman Empire, and the Second World War, everything I have learned about has profoundly fascinated me. History is where you get to learn how the modern world has been shaped today. The more I dived deep into studying history, the more it has sparked the curious side of me. I love visiting historical sites, learning about them, and connecting them to what I have previously learned. Overall, learning history is always fun, and it helps you understand the world better.
